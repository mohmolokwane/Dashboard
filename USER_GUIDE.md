## 🚀 GET STARTED IN 3 MINUTES

### Step 1: Open the Dashboard

1. Find the file: `sme-compliance-dashboard.html` in your downloads
2. **Move it to a permanent location:**
   - **Windows:** Create a folder like `C:\My Business Tools\`
   - **Mac:** Create a folder like `Documents/Business Tools/`
   - **Important:** Don't leave it in Downloads folder

3. **Bookmark it:**
   - Right-click the file → "Create Shortcut" (Windows) or "Make Alias" (Mac)
   - Put the shortcut on your Desktop
   - OR: Open in browser → Bookmark the page (Ctrl+D / Cmd+D)

### Step 2: Set Up Your First Company

1. Double-click to open the dashboard
2. Fill in your company details:

**Company Information:**
- **Company Name:** Exactly as registered with CIPC
- **Registration Number:** Format: 2020/123456/07
- **Registration Date:** The date CIPC registered your company

**Tax Information:**
- **Financial Year-End:** The LAST month of your financial year
   - Example: If year runs Jan-Dec, select "December"
   - Example: If year runs Mar-Feb, select "February"

- **Annual Turnover:** Select your turnover bracket
   - Under R1M
   - R1M-R10M (EME)
   - R10M-R50M (QSE)
   - Over R50M

- **Employees:** Total number (including yourself)
   - If sole proprietor with no staff, enter "1"
   - If you have 5 staff members, enter "5"

- **VAT Registered?** Yes or No
   - If Yes, select filing frequency:
     - **Monthly:** Turnover > R30M/year
     - **Bi-monthly:** Turnover R1.5M-R30M/year
     - **Quarterly:** Special cases (rare)

- **Provisional Taxpayer?** Usually Yes if:
   - Taxable income > R50,000/year OR
   - Turnover > R1 million/year
   - Check with your accountant if unsure

- **Company Type:**
   - Private Company (Pty) Ltd (most common)
   - Public Company (Ltd)
   - NPC (non-profit)

3. Click "Create Company"

**Done!** All your deadlines are now calculated automatically.

**Add a new company:**

## 📊 UNDERSTANDING YOUR DEADLINES

### SARS Module

**Provisional Tax:**
- 1st payment: Due within 6 months of financial year start
- 2nd payment: Due at financial year-end
- Example: Feb year-end → payments due Aug 31 & Feb 28

**VAT Returns:**
- Due 25th of the month after the tax period
- Example: January VAT due Feb 25
- Frequency depends on your turnover

**PAYE (if you have employees):**
- Due 7th of the month following pay period
- Example: January salaries → PAYE due Feb 7
- File even if R0 (nil return)

**Income Tax Return (ITR14):**
- Due 12 months after financial year-end
- Example: Feb year-end → ITR14 due by Feb 28 next year

### CIPC Module

**Annual Return (CoR 30.1):**
- Due 30 **business days** after your registration anniversary
- Example: Registered April 15 → due approximately May 25
- Business days = excludes weekends and public holidays
- Penalty: R50/month if late

### POPIA Module

**10-Point Readiness Checklist:**
- Information Officer appointed and registered
- Privacy Policy published (website/office)
- Data Processing Agreements with third parties
- Data Breach Response Plan documented
- Employee training on POPIA completed
- DSAR procedure (how you handle access requests)
- Security measures (encryption, passwords, access control)
- Impact Assessments for high-risk processing
- Processing Register (what data you collect and why)
- Consent mechanisms (opt-in for marketing, etc.)

**Your Score:**
- 0-3/10: High risk (urgent action needed)
- 4-6/10: Partial compliance (needs work)
- 7-9/10: Good (few gaps to close)
- 10/10: Fully compliant

## ❓ COMMON QUESTIONS

### "Why is my CIPC date so late?"

CIPC allows 30 **business days** (not calendar days) after your registration anniversary. Weekends and public holidays don't count. This means the deadline is usually 6-8 weeks after your anniversary - which is correct.

### "I'm not a provisional taxpayer but it's showing dates?"

Go to company dropdown → ➕ (add company) → Edit your settings. OR delete and re-create company with correct "Provisional Taxpayer" selection.

### "VAT dates are wrong?"

Check your VAT filing frequency:
- **Monthly:** Turnover > R30M/year
- **Bi-monthly:** Turnover R1.5M-R30M/year

If you're unsure, ask your accountant which frequency SARS assigned you.

### "Can I add custom deadlines?"

Not yet - this version only calculates statutory SARS/CIPC deadlines. Custom reminders (e.g., insurance renewals) are coming in v3.

### "Does it send email reminders?"

Not yet - this version requires you to check the dashboard manually. Email/SMS reminders are coming in v3. For now, use the calendar export to set your own alerts.

### "Can my team access this?"

Current version: Each person opens the file on their own computer. Future version (v3) will have cloud sync and team access.

### "What if compliance laws change?"

We'll send updated HTML files when laws change. You'll download the new file and your data migrates automatically (same LocalStorage).

## 🆘 TROUBLESHOOTING

### Problem: Dashboard won't open

**Solution:**
- Try different browser (Chrome recommended)
- Make sure file has `.html` extension
- Don't open from email - save to computer first

### Problem: Data disappeared

**Cause:** Browser cache was cleared OR using private browsing mode

**Solution:**
- Don't use Incognito/Private mode for regular use
- Re-enter company details (takes 3 min)
- Export calendar as backup going forward

### Problem: Dates look wrong

**Check:**
1. Is your financial year-end correct?
2. Is your registration date accurate?
3. Are you in the right company? (check dropdown)

**Fix:** Delete company (🗑️) and re-create with correct details

### Problem: Checkboxes don't work

**Solution:**
- Refresh page (F5 or Cmd+R)
- Try different browser
- Make sure JavaScript is enabled
